How to boil eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • water
  • sauce pan

Instructions

  1. Wash the sauce pan.
  2. Take 250ml water in the sauce pan.
  3. Wash the eggs and put it in the sauce pan.
  4. Ligth the stove.
  5. Put the sauce pan on the stove.
  6. Set timer for 10 mins.
  7. Let it boil
  8. after 10 mins, replace the water with room tempreture water.
  9. Peel the eggs to reveal soft boiled eggs.
